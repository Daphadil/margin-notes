Something Wrong

I Need to Write This Down

by Mara  ·  6 readers

I went into the archive today. I don't know what made me do it — I don't usually go back, I mostly just write forward and let the old posts sit. But I went back today and I found something that I need to write down before I convince myself I imagined it.

There are posts in the archive I didn't write.

Not all of them. The ones I recognize are mine — the ones from 2021, 2022, last year, this year. But there are others. Older ones. They go back to 2009. The voice is close to mine but not quite — like someone who read everything I've ever written and then tried to continue it. Or like something that was writing me, and kept writing when I wasn't there yet.

The details in them are wrong in the right ways. A fourth floor apartment. High ceilings. A plant. A neighbor who is surprised to see them at the door. One of the entries mentions a plant named Gerald.

I named him Gerald in March. I didn't tell anyone that. I didn't write it down until this blog.

I looked at the About page. The one I wrote when I started this, three years ago. The one I thought said: "This is a place for small things. Started in 2021 by Mara, who moved to a new city and needed somewhere to put things down."

It doesn't say that. It has never said that. I don't know how I read it differently for three years. I don't know how I read it at all.

I think I have to leave. I think I have to leave right now, before I forget that I thought that.

I'm going to publish this and then I'm going to go.
